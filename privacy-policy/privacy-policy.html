Privacy Policy

Intro

Welcome to Flow Studio's website! At Flow Studio, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide when you visit our website or interact with our services. We value the trust you place in us and strive to be transparent about our data practices. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy or data security, please don't hesitate to contact us. Thank you for choosing Flow Studio.

What data the website collects from the user:

Flow Studio collects personal information such as name, email address, and any additional information voluntarily provided by users through contact forms, newsletter subscriptions, or other interactive features on our website. Additionally, non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and referring website addresses may be collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies.

When the organization or entity collects personal information:

Personal information is collected when users interact with our website, including but not limited to filling out forms, subscribing to newsletters, or engaging with interactive features.

How the organization or entity uses personal information:

Flow Studio uses personal information to communicate with users, provide requested services, send newsletters or promotional materials (if opted in by the user), and improve the overall user experience on our website. We do not sell, rent, or lease personal information to third parties.

If the website uses cookies:

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and collect information about how the website is used. Users can control cookie preferences through their browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of the website.

Who else has access to the data collected:

Flow Studio may share personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to our users. These third parties are contractually obligated to keep personal information confidential and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

How the company or organization will protect the user’s data:

Flow Studio is committed to protecting the security of user data and upholding its legal responsibilities regarding data use. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Additionally, we regularly review our data processing practices to ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws.
