Intro

Flow Studio was honored to receive an invitation from McDonald's last year to collaborate on its global crew training initiative, The Crew Academy.

Revolutionizing The Learning Experience

At Flow, we thrive on challenges that push the boundaries of creativity and innovation. When McDonald's approached us with the task of reimagining their learning experience, we knew it was an opportunity to revolutionize e-learning for new generations.

McDonald's sought a solution, that would resonate with today's learners, engaging them through various channels and technologies. Drawing upon our expertise in digital media, we embarked on a journey to transform their Crew Academy into an immersive and uplifting experience.

(01)

The Watch Experience

Our approach was multifaceted, drawing upon years of expertise in video production and VFX to capture the vibrant and uplifting environment of McDonald's.

Incorporating a blend of video, interactive games, and cutting-edge technologies, we crafted a learning system that not only educates but also entertains. By harnessing the power of these mediums, we transformed mundane training materials into captivating experiences, fostering a sense of excitement and curiosity among McDonald's crew members.

(02)

The Gamification

The result? A dynamic and fun-filled learning environment that redefines the career path within McDonald's. Through our collaboration, we infused new elements of gamification into every aspect of the learning experience. From interactive quests to virtual simulations, our solution transforms mundane tasks into engaging challenges, making learning enjoyable and effortless for McDonald's crew members.

(03)

The Concept Art

Our approach was multifaceted, leveraging our expertise in concept art and character design to bring forth charismatic characters akin to the beloved McDonald's crew. These characters became the digital cast of our learning system, embodying diversity and inclusion. Drawing inspiration from various cultures and locations, we meticulously crafted each character with unique characteristics, ensuring representation and relatability for McDonald's crew members worldwide.

Incorporating a blend of video, interactive games, and cutting-edge technologies, we seamlessly integrated these diverse characters into the learning experience. Their presence not only adds a layer of authenticity but also fosters a sense of belonging and cultural appreciation among learners. Through our collaboration, we transformed traditional training into a celebration of diversity, making it dynamic and enriching for all.

(04)

A New Face

Even on traditional channels like print and web, we were determined to transcend the ordinary and cultivate an immersive learning experience that marries education with entertainment. Our goal was to captivate learners through materials that not only impart knowledge but also spark joy and curiosity.

Embracing the power of storytelling and editorial design, we crafted engaging narratives and visually stunning content that transport learners into a world of imagination and discovery. From vibrant illustrations to compelling narratives, every element was meticulously designed to elevate the learning journey, making it both enriching and enjoyable. Through our dedication to pushing boundaries, we transformed conventional learning mediums into dynamic portals of exploration and excitement.
